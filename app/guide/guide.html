01 · WHAT IT IS

What exactly is this service

Who uses it

20–35 foreign FIT travelers. Anyone coming to Korea — English speakers drawn by K-drama or K-pop, Japanese and Chinese travelers here for food and culture, and everyone across Asia in between. One thing in common: "I don't want a package, solo has its limits, and I wish I had one person who actually knew the city."

What you get

Starting 2 days before your trip, you connect with a same-age Korean friend over messenger (KakaoTalk / Line / WhatsApp / email). You share what you like, what you want to eat, where you want to go — and your trip quietly begins 2 days early. On the actual day, pick half-day (6h+), full-day (10–12h), or two days with that friend. There's no fixed course — your friend builds it around you, the way they'd take a real friend around.

What it isn't

Not a fixed route. Nobody's holding a flag. No overlapping groups on the same day — one buddy, one party (up to 2 people, same price) for the whole day. Not a random friend-matching app either — the founder personally interviews and selects every buddy, and rematching is free if anything feels off. Legally and structurally, YUKO is "Cultural experience coordination" — connecting, designing, and guaranteeing a day with a peer local friend.

05 · WHY PAID

Why it's paid, not "just a friend-exchange"

"It's just connecting friends — why pay?"

The question we get most. The answer is simple.

Free doesn't last.

Anyone who's used a free friend-matching app (MEEFF, Tandem) knows. You match with 5 people and maybe 1 actually meets you. You grab one coffee and that's it. Next time, "sorry, too busy" comes back.

Free means neither side gets serious. It becomes "we'll meet if we feel like it", not a real appointment. And honestly — even friends aren't easy to ask to block out a whole day for a stranger from abroad.

At YUKO, your buddy prepares the conversation 2 days ahead and blocks out the full day for you. That's not a favor — it's a service-level commitment. The moment money moves, both sides take it seriously, and that's what creates the quality of the experience.

What we actually sell isn't "friend connection." It's "a day designed and guaranteed, just for you."

  • Course research and flow designed around your taste
  • Removing friction — reservations, language, local prices, transport
  • Company by your side the whole day
  • Safety, identity verification, insurance coverage
  • Free rematch if you're not satisfied

All of it together, at that price. Not "getting a friend introduced," but one person taking on every annoying part of a trip for you. That's why we sell coordination, not friendship.
